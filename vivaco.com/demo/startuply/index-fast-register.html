WHAT WE DO FOR YOU

PRODUCT STRATEGY

We will develop the product strategy for your game, identifying key attributes necessary to achieve success.

DISTRIBUTION

We will leverage our existing communities and build new ones, to ensure optimal distribution of your game, ensuring it reaches its target audience.

MONITIZATION

We will help you build the best models, strategies and ad networks that will make your game most profitable.

LOCALIZATION

we will assist in translating your game into multiple languages to appeal to different audiences and global markets.

MARKETING

We will help in creating promotional materials, engage communities and also handle the social media marketing for your game.

APP STORE OPTIMIZATION

We will implement strategies that will push your game up the charts and get it featured on the app store.

PUBLIC RELATIONS

We will help to build and maintain a favourable public image for your game.

COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

We will help to build a community around your game, manage player insights and identify improvement opportunities
